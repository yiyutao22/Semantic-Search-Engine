Get the Luddy student perspective

Curious about life at the Luddy School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering? Our Student Ambassadors will tell you all about it. Check out their bios and email them directly with your questions or to set up a one-on-one chat or Zoom.

You can also learn about Luddy life from our Pacesetters. They help ensure that the student experience here is diverse and inclusive.

Become an Ambassador

Luddy Ambassadors talk to high school students, parents, and current IU students about our school. They host students who visit Luddy, and also go to high schools and events like robotics competitions.

By becoming an Ambassador, you’ll build your leadership and communication skills, add to your resume, network with students and employers, and give back to your school.

Apply

For more information or if you have questions about the program, contact Lauren Headley at lheadley@indiana.edu.

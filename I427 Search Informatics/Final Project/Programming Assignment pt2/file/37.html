Undergraduate Advising and Tutoring

Your advisor is an expert on three things: Luddy, IU, and you

Your advisor is dedicated to helping you meet your goals. Current Luddy majors have an assigned advisor, and we have two advisors for exploratory students, pre-Luddy majors, and students earning a Luddy minor or certificate.

Accessing Luddy Undergraduate Academic Advising Services During Coronavirus COVID-19

The Luddy Academic Advising team is available for email and/or virtual advising sessions throughout the fall 2020 semester. Check your email regularly for specific instructions from your Luddy undergraduate academic advisor.

Current Luddy Undergraduate majors, transfers, and returning students

If you’re a current student and you’ve met the requirements to officially enter Luddy, you can make an appointment with your personal Luddy advisor or email your advisor with questions. Before you meet with your advisor, see your Academic Advisement Report to check on your degree progress.

These advisors also work with admitted transfer students and returning students.

Exploratory students, pre-Luddy majors, and other students

Let’s talk about you, your goals, and how Luddy can help you fulfill them. Our associate directors of academic and career planning work with many kinds of students:

  • Exploratory IU students
  • Students in University Division who are working toward meeting the requirements to officially enter our school
  • Students who are earning a Luddy minor or certificate
  • Students who are considering changing to or adding a Luddy major, minor, or certificate

Tutoring for undergraduates

Our Tutoring Center offers free peer tutoring in core Luddy courses for Luddy majors and nonmajors. The Luddy School has partnered with Knack to provide students with access to campus tutors. Students looking for additional assistance outside of the classroom are advised to consider working with a peer tutor through Knack. To view available tutors, visit indiana.joinknack.com and sign in with your student account.

If you have questions or want to become a tutor, contact Tiana Iruoje at ltutors@indiana.edu .
